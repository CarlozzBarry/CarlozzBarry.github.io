Story

The world has been nearly totally desertified, and only a small portion of it has been spared from this absolute destruction. You, the player, are one of the last survivors of a decimated humanity, thus bearing the burden of recreating a living and pleasant enviroment, for the Earth shall regain liveability and a flourishing vegetation.

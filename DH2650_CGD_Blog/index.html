DESERTIFICATION

STORY
~~~~~~~~~~

The world has been nearly totally desertified, and only a small portion of it has been spared from this absolute destruction. You, the player, are one of the last survivors of a decimated humanity, thus bearing the burden of recreating a living and pleasant enviroment, for the Earth shall regain liveability and a flourishing vegetation.

A LITTLE GAMEPLAY
~~~~~~~~~~

GAME DYNAMICS
~~~~~~~~~~

”EROSION, DESERTIFICATION AND POLLUTION HAVE BECOME OUR LOT. IT IS A WEIRD FORM OF SUICIDE, FOR WE ARE BLEEDING OUR OWN PLANET TO DEATH.”


Desertification is a third-person exploration game centered in a world destroyed by the rapid growth of the desert. You, the player, are the last survivor of an almost extinct population, with the mission of recreating a living environment. Along the way, players can uncover artifacts and stories from the extinct population, and perhaps even bring them back to life.

TECHNICAL FEATURES
~~~~~~~~~~

The game was developed with Unity, and the audio was implemented with Wwise, which allowed the game to host 3D Audio. Audio tracks were both live recorded and fetched from copyright-free sources - e.g. Freesound.org. In terms of image quality, a specific bitrate was chosen, in order to obtain a high resolution effect. For more specific informations, we suggest to go through the Game Design Document, available at this link.

THE SONIFYING HOURGLASS

BACKGROUND
~~~~~~~~~~

Air pollution in offices represents a compelling issue. Good or bad air quality in a closed environment has proven to be a determining factor on the wellness, productivity and health of people working in said environment. This project's aim is to investigate a way of making this people aware of the air condition, using sonification as the mean.

PROTOTYPE DYNAMICS
~~~~~~~~~~

The Sonifying Hourglass is meant to be used in an everyday office setting, as if it were a common gadget sitting on a clerk's desk. The mechanics, as they are meant to be, expect the user to periodically rotate the hourglass, which triggers a sound design increasing in pitch as the hourglass's low side fills up. In the meanwhile, the air pollution sensor gets info about the concentration of CO2 in the surrounding air environment, triggering another sound design in the moment it gets above the safety threshold.

TECHNICAL FEATURES
~~~~~~~~~~

The project, at the moment, works with Arduino boards, the computer's built-in speakers and an air pollution sensor
